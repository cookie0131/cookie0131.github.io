Linux - sed

发表于 2009-05-05   |   分类于 Linux   |  

调用sed

a.使用sed命令行格式为：
sed [选项] sed命令 输入文件
在命令行使用sed命令时，实际命令要加单引号。

b.使用sed脚本文件，格式为：
sed [选项] -f sed脚本文件 输入文件

c.使用第一行具有sed命令解释器的sed脚本文件，其格式为：
sed 脚本文件 [选项] 输入文件

sed选项如下：
n 不打印
p 打印
c 下一命令是编辑命令
f 如果正在调用sed脚本文件，使用此选项。此选项通知s e d一个脚本文件支持所有的s e d
命令，例如：sed -f myscript.sed input_file，这里m y s c r i p t . s e d即为支持s e d命令的文件。

保存sed输出

使用那个重定向
sed ‘some-sed command’ input-file > myoutfile

使用sed在文件按中查询文本的方式

（使用sed在文件中定位文本的方式）
x x为一行号，如1
x , y 表示行号范围从x到y，如2，5表示从第2行到第5行
/ p a t t e r n / 查询包含模式的行。例如/ d i s k /或/[a-z]/
/ p a t t e r n / p a t t e r n / 查询包含两个模式的行。例如/ d i s k / d i s k s /
p a t t e r n / , x 在给定行号上查询包含模式的行。如/ r i b b o n / , 3
x , / p a t t e r n / 通过行号和模式查询匹配行。3 . / v d u /
x , y ! 查询不包含指定行号x和y的行。1 , 2 !

基本sed编辑命令

p 打印匹配行
= 显示文件行号
a \ 在定位行号后附加新文本信息
i \ 在定位行号后插入新文本信息
d 删除定位行
c \ 用新文本替换定位文本
s 使用替换模式替换相应模式
r 从另一个文件中读文本
w 写文本到一个文件
q 第一个模式匹配完成后推出或立即推出
l 显示与八进制A S C I I代码等价的控制字符
{ } 在定位行执行的命令组
n 从另一个文件中读文本下一行，并附加在下一行
g 将模式2粘贴到/pattern n/
y 传送字符
n 延续到下一输入行；允许跨行的模式匹配语句

sed和正则表达式

如果要定位一特殊字符，必须使用（ \）屏蔽其特殊含义

[unicom@localhost test1]$ more quote.txt
The honeysuckle band played all night long for only $90.
It was an evening of splendid music and conpany.
Too bad the disco floor fell through at 23:10.
The local nurse Miss P.Neave was in attendance.

基本sed编程举例

  1. 使用p（rint）显示行
    [unicom@localhost test1]$ sed ‘2p’ quote.txt
    The honeysuckle band played all night long for only $90.
    It was an evening of splendid music and conpany.
    It was an evening of splendid music and conpany.
    Too bad the disco floor fell through at 23:10.
    The local nurse Miss P.Neave was in attendance.
  1. 打印范围
    [unicom@localhost test1]$ sed -n ‘1,3p’ quote.txt
    The honeysuckle band played all night long for only $90.
    It was an evening of splendid music and conpany.
    Too bad the disco floor fell through at 23:10.
  1. 打印模式
    [unicom@localhost test1]$ sed -n ‘/Neave/‘p quote.txt
    The local nurse Miss P.Neave was in attendance.
  1. 使用模式和行号进行查询
    [unicom@localhost test1]$ sed -n ‘/The/‘p quote.txt
    The honeysuckle band played all night long for only $90.
    The local nurse Miss P.Neave was in attendance.

[unicom@localhost test1]$ sed -n ‘4,/The/‘p quote.txt
The local nurse Miss P.Neave was in attendance.

  1. 匹配元字符
    [unicom@localhost test1]$ sed -n ‘/\$/‘p quote.txt
    The honeysuckle band played all night long for only $90.

  2. 显示整个文件
    [unicom@localhost test1]$ sed -n ‘1,$p’ quote.txt
    The honeysuckle band played all night long for only $90.
    It was an evening of splendid music and conpany.
    Too bad the disco floor fell through at 23:10.
    The local nurse Miss P.Neave was in attendance.

  3. 任意字符
    匹配任意字母，后跟任意字母的0次或多次重复，并以i n g结尾，模式为/ . i n g /。可以使用
    这个模式查询以i n g结尾的任意单词。
    [unicom@localhost test1]$ sed -n ‘/.    ing/‘p quote.txt
    It was an evening of splendid music and conpany.

  1. 首行
    [unicom@localhost test1]$ sed -n ‘1p’ quote.txt
    The honeysuckle band played all night long for only $90.
  1. 最后一行
    要打印最后一行，使用$。$是代表最后一行的元字符。
    [unicom@localhost test1]$ sed -n ‘$p’ quote.txt
    The local nurse Miss P.Neave was in attendance.
  1. 打印行号
    要打印行号，使用等号=。打印模式匹配的行号，使用格式/ p a t t e r n / =。
    [unicom@localhost test1]$ sed -e ‘/music/=’ quote.txt
    The honeysuckle band played all night long for only $90.
    2
    It was an evening of splendid music and conpany.
    Too bad the disco floor fell through at 23:10.
    The local nurse Miss P.Neave was in attendance.

[unicom@localhost test1]$ sed -n ‘/music/=’ quote.txt
2

  1. 删除文本
    sed删除文本格式：
    [address[,address]]d

删除第一行
[unicom@localhost test1]$ sed ‘1d’ quote.txt
It was an evening of splendid music and conpany.
Too bad the disco floor fell through at 23:10.
The local nurse Miss P.Neave was in attendance.

删除1〜3行
[unicom@localhost test1]$ sed ‘1,3d’ quote.txt
The local nurse Miss P.Neave was in attendance.

删除最后一行
[unicom@localhost test1]$ sed ‘$d’ quote.txt
The honeysuckle band played all night long for only $90.
It was an evening of splendid music and conpany.
Too bad the disco floor fell through at 23:10.

也可以使用正则表达式进行删除
[unicom@localhost test1]$ sed ‘/Neave/d’ quote.txt
The honeysuckle band played all night long for only $90.
It was an evening of splendid music and conpany.
Too bad the disco floor fell through at 23:10.

  1. 替换文本
    替换命令用替换模式替换指定模式，格式为：
    [address[,address]] s/pattern-to-find /replacement-pattern/[g p w n]
    s选项通知s e d这是一个替换操作，并查询p a t t e r n - t o - f i n d，成功后用r e p l a c e m e n t - p a t t e r n替
    换它。
    替换选项如下：
    g 缺省情况下只替换第一次出现模式，使用g选项替换全局所有出现模式。
    p 缺省s e d将所有被替换行写入标准输出，加p选项将使- n选项无效。- n选项不打印输出
    结果。
    w 文件名使用此选项将输出定向到一个文件。

替换n i g h t为N I G H T
[unicom@localhost test1]$ sed ‘s/night/NIGHT/‘ quote.txt
The honeysuckle band played all NIGHT long for only $90.
It was an evening of splendid music and conpany.
Too bad the disco floor fell through at 23:10.
The local nurse Miss P.Neave was in attendance.

要进行全局替换，即替换所有出现模式，只需在命令后加g选项。下面的例子将所有T h e
替换成Wo w！。
[unicom@localhost test1]$ sed ‘s/The/Wow!/g’ quote.txt
Wow! honeysuckle band played all night long for only $90.
It was an evening of splendid music and conpany.
Too bad the disco floor fell through at 23:10.
Wow! local nurse Miss P.Neave was in attendance.

将替换结果写入一个文件用w选项，下面的例子将s p l e n d i d替换为S P L E N D I D的替换结果
写入文件s e d . o u t
[unicom@localhost test1]$ sed ‘s/splendid/SPLNDID/w sed.out’ quote.txt
The honeysuckle band played all night long for only $90.
It was an evening of SPLNDID music and conpany.
Too bad the disco floor fell through at 23:10.
The local nurse Miss P.Neave was in attendance.

使用替换修改字符串

如果要附加或修改一个字符串，可以使用（ &）命令，&命令保存发现模式以便重新调用
它，然后把它放在替换字符串里面。
[unicom@localhost test1]$ sed -n ‘s/nurse/“HELLO” &/p’ quote.txt
The local “HELLO” nurse Miss P.Neave was in attendance.

将sed结果写入文件命令

[address[,address]]w filename
‘w’选项通知s e d将结果写入文件。f i l e n a m e是自解释文件名
[unicom@localhost test1]$ sed ‘1,2 w filedt’ quote.txt
The honeysuckle band played all night long for only $90.
It was an evening of splendid music and conpany.
Too bad the disco floor fell through at 23:10.
The local nurse Miss P.Neave was in attendance.

匹配后退出

address q
[unicom@localhost test1]$ sed ‘/.a.*/q’ quote.txt
The honeysuckle band played all night long for only $90.
